About Me

Hi, I’m Kathryn. I have a silver tabby cat called Professor Minerva McGonagall, and have just reserved a black kitten named Salem to join my magical menagerie in the next week or so. My hobbies include but are not limited to oil painting, crochet, DIY, lifting heavy objects, keeping my house plants alive, and reading fantasy romance novels. I listen to heavy metal and lofi with a sprinkling of soundtracks. I mostly wear monochromatic clothing, but occasionally I leave the house in light mode just to keep my coworkers on their toes. I am currently working as a self-taught Digital Designer and am working towards my goal of switching careers to become a fully-fledged Software Developer in 2024.

Why Do I Want To Be A Developer?

When I was younger, I wanted to be a game developer. After several failed attempts to create a game by myself, I decided I wasn’t smart enough to be a developer, and taught myself graphic design instead. As the years went on, I learnt UX/UI and web design using Adobe XD and the Elementor plugin for WordPress and built a new website for my current company. I began programming again, and discovered how I learn best. I love solving problems and challenging myself, and have learnt more by completing my application projects than ever before. Building a functioning game made me realise that becoming a software developer is more achievable than I had originally thought, and I am excited to make the switch and do something I love for a living.
